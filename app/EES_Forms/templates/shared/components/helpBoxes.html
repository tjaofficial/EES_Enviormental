Packets are an easy way to put forms into organized groups. These packets can be used to print off groups of "submitted" inspection forms. To add a new packet, enter in a new "Packet Name" and click "save". Once saved, you will see a blue bar appear with the new packet name. You can drag-and-drop forms from the top into the packets or you can click "add" to to advance to the add page. You can click the blue bar to expand the packet and see the existing forms within. To the right of the packet you will see the settings or delete icons.
